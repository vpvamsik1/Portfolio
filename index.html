About Me

Vamsi Krishna Pappusetti is an ASU alumni seeking a career in web development. He graduated with a degree in Justice Studies in May 2017 with the intention of going to law school, but changed course to enter the software industry upon graduating.

He enrolled in an online web development course on Udemy in July 2017. After working in a few law related and sales jobs, he joined a coding bootcamp at UCLA extension in the May of 2018. Here he learned multiple coding languages and created numerous projects.

He has a great deal of passion for making aesthetically pleasing works that he hopes to incorporate into his websites.
